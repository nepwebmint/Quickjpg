Frequently Asked Questions

How does QuickJPG convert images?

QuickJPG uses client-side JavaScript and HTML5 Canvas to process your images directly in your browser. This means your images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and fast conversion speeds.

What image formats are supported?

Currently, QuickJPG supports conversion from PNG and WEBP formats to JPG. We're continuously working to add support for more formats in future updates.

Is there a file size limit?

Since processing happens in your browser, very large images might slow down the conversion process. For optimal performance, we recommend images under 10MB.

Are my images stored anywhere?

No, your images are processed entirely in your browser and never uploaded to any server. This ensures complete privacy and security for your files.

Is QuickJPG completely free to use?

Yes, QuickJPG is completely free with no hidden costs or limitations. We believe in providing accessible tools for everyone.
